# Statement of AI Usage & Collaborative Research
**Researcher:** Dylan Cabrera Giler  
**Framework:** Human-AI Collaborative Engineering  

## Research Philosophy
In alignment with the goal of contributing to **Quantum Information Science** at **Argonne National Laboratory**, this repository utilizes Artificial Intelligence as a specialized tool for **Externalized Cognition**. AI is employed not to replace the researcher’s theoretical understanding, but to optimize **Output Throughput** by mitigating the systemic bottlenecks associated with **Executive Dysfunction**.

## Functional Applications
* **Code Scaffolding:** AI assisted in the structural generation of complex Qiskit circuits, including **Shor’s 9-Qubit Code** and **Quantum Phase Estimation**.
* **Debugging & Optimization:** AI served as a technical peer for troubleshooting **Ubuntu 24.04** environments and optimizing **ADAM/Momentum** hyperparameters.
* **Documentation Architecture:** AI assisted in organizing high-density technical summaries and maintaining the **Application Tracker** for academic transitions.

## Ethical Boundaries & Integrity
* **Theoretical Mastery:** Every circuit and algorithm in this repository has been vetted for physical and logical accuracy by the researcher.
* **Academic Independence:** All proctored assessments, including the **ALEKS Math Placement (Baseline: 39)**, are completed without AI assistance to ensure an honest representation of mathematical foundations.
* **Transparency:** This statement serves as an audit trail for admissions committees at **Wilbur Wright College** and **UIUC**.
